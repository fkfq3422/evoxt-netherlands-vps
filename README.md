## Why Netherlands? The Amsterdam Advantage Explained

Before we get into Evoxt specifically, it's worth understanding why Amsterdam keeps showing up in "best European VPS location" lists.

The Netherlands sits at a geographic sweet spot — right in the middle of Western Europe, with excellent fiber links going out in every direction. Amsterdam hosts **AMS-IX**, one of the world's largest internet exchange points, connecting over 875 networks from 70+ countries. What that means practically: when your server in Amsterdam sends a packet to someone in London, Berlin, or Paris, it's not bouncing through three middlemen. It goes direct.

The result is consistently **sub-20ms latency to 95% of European internet users**, which matters enormously for things like:

- Web applications where page load speed affects bounce rate
- Game servers where 50ms vs 15ms is literally the difference between winning and losing
- APIs and microservices where round-trip time compounds across calls
- Businesses serving EU customers who need GDPR-compliant infrastructure in European jurisdiction

Evoxt's Netherlands node connects to **AMS-IX, ERA-IX, and NL-IX** — that's three separate exchange points, giving you routing flexibility that most budget providers don't bother with.
